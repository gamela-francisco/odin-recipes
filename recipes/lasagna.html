Lasagna

Description

his classic lasagna recipe is a comforting, hearty dish that layers rich, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly cooked pasta sheets. Start by simmering a flavorful tomato sauce with ground beef, onions, garlic, and aromatic herbs like basil and oregano, allowing the flavors to meld together. Next, prepare a smooth ricotta filling mixed with fresh spinach for a burst of flavor. Layer the sauce, pasta, and cheese mixture, then bake until golden and bubbling. The result is a decadent, melt-in-your-mouth lasagna with layers of gooey cheese and savory goodness that’s perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying a family meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6.5 ounce) cans crushed tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
